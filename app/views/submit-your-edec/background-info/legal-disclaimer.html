NHS England and NHS Improvement, as with all NHS organisations is required to share intelligence with other statutory bodies, both in circumstances where they have a legal right to request it e.g. National Audit Office, CQC; or where it is necessary or expedient for them to receive it in order to protect the welfare of individuals or to discharge their functions.

Practices are therefore reminded of the significance to ensure that responses provided to questions are accurate and can withstand legal scrutiny, the declaration is treated and considered to be a formal submission once declared.
